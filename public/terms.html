Static QR codes

Scan by Arxeni creates static QR codes. A static QR code does not contain an expiration date from this app. However, the QR code points to the text, URL, or QR Hub data you generated. If the destination URL breaks, changes, becomes private, or expires, the scan experience may stop working.

Your responsibility

You are responsible for the content you encode, the destinations you link to, the logos you upload, and the QR codes you publish or print. Do not use the tool to create misleading, harmful, unlawful, abusive, infringing, or deceptive QR codes.

QR Hub usage

QR Hub mode creates a static landing page URL with destination data encoded in the URL hash. It is not a private storage system. Anyone with the QR code or URL may be able to access the destinations included in that hub.

Design and scan reliability

Custom colors, transparent backgrounds, logos, gradients, and custom body shapes can affect scannability. Always test your QR code on multiple devices before printing, publishing, packaging, or using it in production.

For best results, keep strong contrast, use a sufficient quiet zone, avoid oversized logos, and use higher error correction when adding a logo or more complex styling.

QR identification

QR identification is provided to help decode QR images or camera input. Decoded content should be reviewed before opening links. Scan by Arxeni does not guarantee that decoded destinations are safe, accurate, or trustworthy.
