# Scan by Arxeni

Scan by Arxeni is a free QR code studio for creating static QR codes, QR hub links, and styled QR exports.

It is built for people who need a clean QR code they can download, print, share, or place on menus, posters, packaging, events, profiles, and business materials.

## What You Can Do

- Generate a static QR code from any text or URL.
- Create a QR Hub with multiple destinations from one QR code.
- Style your QR code with presets, colors, gradients, custom shapes, and a logo.
- Export your QR code as PNG or SVG.
- Download QR codes with a normal or transparent background.
- Identify an existing QR code by uploading an image.
- Scan a QR code with your camera when your browser allows camera access.

## Static QR Codes

Scan by Arxeni creates static QR codes. That means the QR code itself does not expire.

A static QR code can still stop working if the destination it points to stops working. For example, a QR code that points to a deleted webpage, expired file, private link, or broken URL will no longer give people a useful scan experience.

For the best long-term result:

- Use a destination URL you control.
- Keep the linked page online.
- Test the QR code before printing.
- Avoid changing or deleting the destination after sharing the QR code.

## QR Hub

QR Hub lets one QR code open a small landing page with multiple links.

You can add destinations such as:

- Website
- Facebook
- Instagram
- Menu
- Contact card

Only the destinations you fill in will appear on the hub page.

## Styling Options

You can customize the look of your QR code with:

- Visual presets
- Code and background colors
- Gradient accent color
- Transparent background
- Logo upload
- Body shape
- Outer eye shape
- Inner eye shape
- Quiet zone
- Export size
- Error correction level

Styled QR codes should always be tested before printing. Strong contrast and enough quiet zone space make QR codes easier to scan.

## Downloads

Use **PNG** for normal sharing, social posts, documents, and most printing needs.

Use **SVG** when you need a scalable file for design tools, large-format printing, or layouts where the QR code may be resized.

Use **Transparent background** when you want to place the QR code over your own design, poster, or branded layout.

## QR Identification

Identify QR helps you decode an existing QR code.

You can:

- Upload a QR image.
- Start the camera scanner.
- Copy the decoded result.
- Open the decoded link.
- Send the result back into the generator.

Always review decoded links before opening them.

## Good Uses

Scan by Arxeni works well for:

- Restaurant menus
- Event posters
- Portfolios
- Social media profiles
- Business cards
- Product packaging
- School or organization forms
- Contact pages
- Static landing pages

## Important Note

Scan by Arxeni does not make broken links permanent. The QR code can be permanent, but the destination must stay alive.
